Childminder

Childminder is someone who cares for children in her own home. Self-employed, she is responsible for her own tax, maternity rights and insurance. Most work from 8am to 6pm but are usually flexible, some offer weekends/overnight and emergency care.

All childminders are regularly inspected by Ofsted (Office for Standars in Education). To view a copy on the Ofsted website, you will need the URN (Unique Reference Number) from the childminder.
