About

This excellent opportunity has arisen under the agreement of the member States of the Council of Europe, "considering that the aim of Council of Europe is to achieve greater unity between its members, in particular for the purpose of facillitating their social progress, noting that in Europe more and more young persons, especially girls, are going abroad to be placed "au pair".

Therefore, the above mentioned placement is the temporary reception by families, in exchange for certain services, of young foreigners who come to improve their linguistic and possibly professional knowledge, as well as their general culture by building up a better knowledge of the country were they are hosted.

Requirements:

  • the person shall not be less than 18 or more than 30years of age (exceptions to the upper age limit may be granted by the competent authority of the receiving country ; if justified).
  • will have a medical certificate, declaring the general state of health.
  • the rights and obligations of the Au Pair and the Family will be the subject of an agreement in writing ( the agreement will specify the manner in which the Au Pair is to share the life of the receiving Family, while at the same time enjoying a specific degree of independence).
  • will join the Family`s services ( consisting in participation in a day-to-day family duties).
  • will have a DBS ( Disclosure & Baring Check) (previously known as CRB check).
  • will have adequate references
  • is caring and eager to learn
  • has a certain level of knowledge of English/ Other languages
  • is an EU member - meets all immigration and other legislation requirements.

Benefits:

  • the Au Pair will receive full board and lodging and will occupy a separate room.
  • will be given adequate time to attend language courses as well as for cultural and professional development.
  • will have at least 1 full free day/week, not less than one such free day in every month being a Sunday, and shall have full opportunity to take part in religious worship.
  • will receive a certain sum of money ( pocket money), the amount of which, and the intervals will be determined by a written agreement.
  • You will be protected by a written agreement and You will have insurance during your stay ( the agreement might include extra benefits other than listed, at the discretion of the hosting Family).
  • all families are fully vetted.
  • You will have social and cultural opportunities.
  • your responsibilities as an Au Pair are limited to Child Care (related duties are set by the local authorities, therefore You will not be expected to perform tasks that would include heaby manual labour ).
  • You will receive a comprehensive WeLcOmE PaCk.
  • Free UK Sim card.
  • full support from Us - 24h Help line for emergency cases.

How to apply?

If You meet the Au Pair Programme requirements, please:

  1. Start by registering on Our website.
  2. We will call You / Skype. If you are successful We will match You with a Family.
  3. We will arrange an interview with the Family ( You are required to interview at least 2 times before You agree. Prepare well and ask all the relevant questions! ).
  4. Once you both decide you are compatible, you will together choose the arrival date.
  5. During the waiting period, you will need to obtain all the relevant paperwork from Us (before You leave).
  6. We will always encourage families to wait for You at the airport.
  7. We will support You with regular phone calls, follow ups and welfare visits.
