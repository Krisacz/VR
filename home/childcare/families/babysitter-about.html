Babysitter 

The type of work for babysitters varies to the babies, from watching a sleeping child, changing nappies, to playing games, preparing meals, teaching the child to read, etc.; depending on the agreement between the parents and the babysitter.

In some countries various organisations produce courses for babysitters, mainly focusing on child safety and first aid for infants and children. Usually, these trainings can be provided at local hospitals. 

In British English the term refers only to caring for a child for a few hours, on an informal basis and usually in the evening when the child is asleep for most of the time.
