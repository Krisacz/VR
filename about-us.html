About Us

Visio Recruitment sourced from the strong passion for high quality service/opportunity, based on a much personalised support to the individual and therefore offering the best candidate/workforce to the employer.

The company offers EU employment & recruitment consultancy services. We pride ourselves on having an excellent international business relation with all of our clients.

Vision and purpose: to support our clients in their development.

We envisage creating good quality relationships, building effective partnerships in order to support our clients.

 

We are experienced in:

  • Childcare
  • Charities & Voluntary work
  • Hospitality & Tourism
  • Retail & Sales
  • Health & Social Care
  • Transport & Logistics
  • IT

 

We desire to be the opportunity for the best candidate - the opportunity to pursue their ambitions, transform their outlook and unlock a pool of talent that will contribute to a prosperous future.

 

Visio Recruitment is IRP (Institute of Recruitment Professionals) member - fully committed to adhere to their Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct:

    1) Respect for Work Relationships
    2) Respect for Honesty and Transparency
    3) Respect for Confidentiality and Privacy
    4) Respect for Laws
    5) Respect for Diversity
    6) Commitment to Professional Development

  • Considerable pool of candidates (national & international)
  • Pre-selection of the candidates, according to your requirements

  • Promptness
