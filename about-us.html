About Us

Visio Recruitment Ltd offers employment and recruitment consultancy services.

The company is based in Greater Manchester, United Kingdom.

We are experienced in Childcare; Charities & Voluntary work; Hospitality & Tourism; Retail & Sales; Health & Social Care; Transport & Logistics; IT Technologies.

 

We offer national & international services, within the European Union. We pride ourselves on having an excellent international business relation with all of our clients.

 

Why Visio Recruitment?

  • Get a professional and personalised service.
  • We offer fair conditions and a reliable, fast & simple service.
  • We will publish your job offer on our website; on social media sites & newspapers (national and/or international).
  • We can count on a considerable pool of candidates (national & international).
  • Pre-selection of the candidates, according to your requirements.
  • Accurate reference checks.
  • Prompt presentation of the chosen candidates.
  • Quality clients & candidates.
  • Consulting & support.

 

We are in contact with various colleges & universities (national & international) for various trainee programs.

 

We are collaborating with Jobcentre Plus to support various schemes:

  • Youth Contract (which helps 18 to 24year olds find work).
  • Work Choice (employing a disabled person who needs specialist support).
  • Apprenticeships (organised via the National Apprenticeship Service and often follow a period of work experience. They combine practical training with study).
  • Work Programme (creating opportunities for people looking for work).
  • Work Clubs.
  • Work Together (Volunteering to improve the skills and job prospects).
  • Sector –based work academies (sector-based training, work experiences and a guaranteed job interview).
